About Us

Founded in March 2023 and based at Andrea Papandreou 35, Marousi, Athens, CD Programming excels in providing technological solutions to non-tech businesses. We specialize in database creation, maintenance, and website development, along with offering expert consulting in ERP, CRM, cloud services, enterprise mobility applications, and electronic invoicing. Our goal is to ensure companies can manage and expand their digital presence seamlessly.
